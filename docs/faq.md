# FAQ

## Is the Bible behind a paywall here?
No. Scripture and core study resources remain free. Support options exist to fund
hosting, translation, security, and tool-building.

## What is different about the GCB approach?
Clarity, structure, and walkable application:
- scripture-first
- cross-linking and indexing
- consistent “Mirror → Water → Fire” discipleship rhythm

## Can I use these tools in my church?
Yes. See [Offers](./offers/index.html) for church packs and training.

## Do you accept volunteers?
Yes. Open issues or contact through the GitHub org.
