# Sponsor a Milestone

Milestones are specific deliverables you can fund:

Examples:
- “Translate weekly plans into 2 new languages”
- “Build a citation validator for repos”
- “Publish 12 weeks of reading plans”
- “Church pack v1 (print-ready PDFs)”

## Rules
- No paywalls for Scripture
- Transparent delivery + updates
- Sponsors can be thanked publicly (optional)
