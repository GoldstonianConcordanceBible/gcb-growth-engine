# Supporter

For individuals who want to keep the work sustainable.

## What support funds
- site + repo maintenance
- new reading plans
- agent tooling
- translation support (as available)

## Support options
- Monthly support (preferred)
- One-time gift
- Sponsor a specific milestone (translation/tooling)

> Supporters receive updates and behind-the-scenes notes, not “more Bible.”
