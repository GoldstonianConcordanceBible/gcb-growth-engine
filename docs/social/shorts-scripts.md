# Shorts Scripts (15–30 sec)

## Template A: Mirror → Water → Fire
- Mirror: “Here’s what the text says…”
- Water: “Here’s what it means…”
- Fire: “Here’s what you do today…”

## Template B: One verse + one action
- “Today’s verse is…”
- “One sentence meaning…”
- “One step of obedience…”

## CTA (keep it clean)
- “If you want the weekly plan, link in bio.”
- “Study tools are in the GitHub.”
- “Scripture first, always.”
